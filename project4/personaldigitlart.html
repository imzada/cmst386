Golden Gate Park Postcard

I like to play around with an old film camera passed down to me by my father. I spend weekends taking photos of beautiful spots in San Francisco and chose to make postcards for the city if I were to ever print and send them out to family and friends. This piece is a special favorite, as it's one of my favorite spots in the city to share with people when they're able to visit. This was edited in Photoshop and finalized in Illustrator.
