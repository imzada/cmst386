Restaurant Redesign

For this project we were tasked with redesigning an existing brochure menu for a local restaurant. I chose a local favorite, something that I was familiar with, so that I would be able to properly design their image.

TerrorRefuge

This is a smaller piece focusing on typography. I was tasked with using minimal color and type to portray two different moods, hence Terror vs. Regufe. The colors and fonts are meant to balance each other out while also contrasting each other.

Style Book

This stylebook is meant to showcase my work in layout design and to show my understanding of layout and basic design heirarchy rules.

Magazine Cover

A personal favorite, we were tasked with creating a magazine spread featuring content of our choice. For this project I chose to create a magazine that would be showcasing Accessibilty design in the real world. It's also meant to be a piece of work that shows how easy and beautiful accessibilty can be to incorporate in design!

Magazine Spread

The rest of this project was also used to showcase skills in layout and typographic design. The pages are structured to make reading easy and the article was handwritten by me to offer insight on how we, as abled bodies, can 'do the work' for disability awareness.
