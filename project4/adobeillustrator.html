Stamp Project

One of my first projects using Adobe Illustrator was to create two new stamps for the mail in a design of my choosing. As I live in San Francisco, a city still cherishing the memories of Summer '69, I decided to go for a retro 60's look. The two stamps use geometric shapes and simple, muted colors to keep them modern as well. I narrowed down my ideas to what the biggest pieces of San Francisco are: the Golden Gate Bridge and Foggy Oceans Landscapes.

Terraforming Mars Poster

A personal favorite in Illustrator, this project was fun to create as Terraforming Mars is my favorite board game. We were asked to come up with, and design, a movie + movie poster.

City Scape

This project was meant to showcase our skills using layers, colors, strokes, and shapes in Illustrator. I chose to directly redesign a photo I had of my bus stop in San Francisco. Each piece of the buildings and other features are different and unique in shape and color.
